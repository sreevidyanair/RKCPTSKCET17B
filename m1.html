    Quick Enquiry

Munnar Catering College is established and managed by Thachankary Foundation – a Trust governed by Indian Trust Act, 1882 and promoted by Royal Group of Companies - a multi facetted conglomerate engaged in Education, Aviation, Shipping, Advertising, Plantation, Communication, Hotel, Resorts, Travel & Tourism Industries. The International Campus of Munnar Catering College is located at 6500 ft. above sea level in Thachankary Hills, Sooryanelli, Munnar, Kerala.

The facilities of the College include helipad, swimming pool, jacuzzi, sauna, steam bath, gymnasium, indoor stadium, cricket pitch, amphi theatre, modern hostels etc. For the convenience of those who cannot study at the main campus at Munnar, the city campuses of Munnar Catering College are established at Cochin and Trivandrum.
